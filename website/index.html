Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish.Perfect for any meal .The classic omelette combine beaten eggs cooked to perfection optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegitables or meats

Preparation Time

  • Total Time :Approximatily 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 Minutes
  • Cooking:5 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt to Taste
  • Pepper to Taste
  • One Table Spoon Of Oil or Butter
  • Optional fillings :cheese,diced vegitables,cooked meat,herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat The Eggs
  2. Heat The Pan
  3. Cooke The Omelette
  4. Add fillings(Optional)
  5. Fold And Serve
  6. Enjoy
